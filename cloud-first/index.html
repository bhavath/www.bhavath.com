STRATEGY:
Cloud-First

Destination: Digital Enterprise

Digitalization is the cause of large-scale transformations across industries, providing unparalleled opportunities for value creation, while representing a major risk of disruption. The emerging digital economy is fueled by digital trends, changing customer expectations, digital technologies, and the pace set by innovative companies taking advantage of these trends.

Rapid innovation and availability of enabling technology

Disruption and competitive edge gained by upstarts and early adopters

New and enhanced digital experiences changing customer expectations and behavior

Increasing competitive pressures and fear of obsolescence across industries

Forcing businesses to embark on digital business transformation to compete in these dynamic markets

Enterprises failing to reimagine their business could lose the competitive advantage they hold in the market. Understanding the impact of these market shifts and successfully evolving the enterprise to avoid or exploit disruption means undertaking a digital transformation journey to reach the digital enterprise destination by achieving these permanent characteristics.

Permanent Speed & Agility

Continuous Services & Product Innovation

Consistent & Connected Experiences

End-to-End Process Optimization

Intelligence-Driven Business Innovation

Enabler: Technology

Technology is a key enabler to the digital transformation journey. We have developed five technology-driven tracks that enterprise should adopt on their digital journey.

Software-Driven Business

Build software in the cloud to compete and stay ahead – by adding new revenue streams, and meeting new business demands through industrialized software delivery at digital speed.

Intelligent Analytics

Drive intelligent business strategies with the cloud – to acquire, organize, and analyze data across sources, size, velocity, and formats for customer engagement, business performance, and smart automation.

IoT Connected Enterprise

Leverage the cloud to monetize data from sensors and devices – for creating new revenue streams, better asset management, smart monitoring, innovate connected experiences, and intelligent processes automation.

Modern Infrastructure

Become a nimble, business centric and modern IT organization with a "service delivery" mindset, smartly leveraging the Cloud to drive better responsiveness and cost efficiencies.

Smart Digital Workplace

Create an empowered workforce with cloud business apps to enable a collaborative and secure digital workplace, delivered across locations & devices, and maximize productivity and innovation.

Strategy: Cloud-First

Of the many digital-enabling technologies, we believe cloud adoption will deliver the widest and deepest impact on how organizations compete in the digital age. While traditional IT organizations look to the cloud as an infrastructure hosting solution, digital organizations are looking at the rich continuum of cloud services as a strategic and foundational digital enabler.

By our measures, cloud adoption is mainstream and growing rapidly, for both traditional IT modernization as well as exploration & innovation.

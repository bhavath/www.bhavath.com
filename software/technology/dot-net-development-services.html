.NET Development

Bhavath provides .NET development services for enterprise-grade software, web and mobile applications leveraging Agile and DevOps practices. We help organizations choose the right architecture (tiered, micro-services, event-driven, other) depending on their scalability, elasticity, maintainability, operability, and other requirements; guide them through platform and tool selections; execute ux-design-development-testing-release; and perform ongoing development and operations.

.NET Experience

We help enterprises and technology driven companies and startups build contemporary applications on the .NET platform. Customers looking to build and deploy applications in the cloud benefit from our experience across the continuum of cloud application platform services for containerization, PaaS and serverless architectures.

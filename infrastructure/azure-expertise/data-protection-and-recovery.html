Azure Data Protection & Recovery

Bhavath implements Microsoft Azure based data protection & recovery solutions to protect enterprise workloads across on-premises, cloud and hybrid environments. Using Microsoft Azure and leading partner solutions, we help businesses build efficient, durable and scalable backups and recovery environments, while addressing security and regulatory compliance requirements.

Expertise

We bring a proven record of developing automation-led, unified and scalable data protection architectures for backup, replication and recovery on Microsoft Azure. We implement solutions for optimized and policy-based backups designed for rapid recovery, simplified management, and cost efficiency.

Cloud DR&BC Strategy

Develop business continuity strategy and establish cloud based disaster recovery plan, framework, checklist and procedures

Regulatory Compliance Management

Implement compliance frameworks to meet regulatory, eDiscovery and other statutory requirements for data in the cloud

Cloud Workload Data Protection

Design efficient, consistent, and scalable backup, replication and recovery for data and workloads hosted on Microsoft Azure

Hybrid Workload Data Protection

Design efficient, consistent, and scalable backup, replication and recovery of on-premises workloads and data to Microsoft Azure

Disaster Recovery Testing

Deliver non-disruptive, automated failover testing with extensive reporting to reduce risk, withstand outage, and meet compliance

Disaster Recovery Automation

Automated failover & failback to reduce recovery time, eliminate complexity or risks, and rapidly deploy business continuity plans

Data Protection Monitoring

Monitor backups and replication to manage configuration or schedules, optimize costs and rapidly troubleshoot issues & risks

Security & Encryption

Secure in-transit and at-rest backup and archival data with encryption, policies and other information security practices
